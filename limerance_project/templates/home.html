Our Coaching Services
Relationship & Dating Coaching

I work with individuals and couples to improve communication, emotional intimacy, and trust. We focus on building confidence, setting goals, and managing boundaries to foster healthier, more secure relationships.

Limerence Coaching

Specializing in limerence, I help individuals manage obsessive thoughts and behaviors tied to unrequited love. Through mindfulness, value-based living, and self-compassion, we work towards relief and a purposeful life.

Why Choose Us?

We provide a compassionate, presence-filled approach to coaching. With a focus on real-time communication improvements and practical assignments, we help you create more secure, trusting, and loving relationships.
