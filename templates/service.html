Frequently Asked Questions

What is limerence?

Limerence is an intense emotional state of obsessive infatuation with another person, characterized by intrusive thoughts and a strong desire for reciprocation.

How do I know if I'm experiencing limerence?

Common signs include constant preoccupation with the person, idealization, mood swings based on interactions with them, and neglecting other aspects of life.

Can limerence be overcome?

Yes, with the right support and strategies, individuals can overcome limerence and develop healthier relationship patterns.

How are your coaching services different from therapy?

Our coaching focuses on present challenges and actionable strategies to move forward, whereas therapy may delve deeper into past experiences and mental health diagnoses.
