Plumeria Package ($175/session)

This package includes weekly coaching sessions, over a period of 4 weeks. During this time, we will explore the meaning of limerence and how it differs from love, signs and symptoms, your specific situation and how it manifests in your day-to-day life, tracking thought patterns, neuropsychological components, mindfulness skills, triggers, coping skills to ride out urges and compulsions, plus ways to replace limerent behavior with positive and meaningful experiences.

Interested
Hibiscus Package ($150/session)

This package includes weekly sessions, over a period of 8 weeks. It is structured similarly to the 4-week package, though it incorporates exploration of childhood experiences and impact on limerence, healthy relationship skills, and further mindfulness practice. This package will also include use of an assessment tool to measure progress in week 1 and 8.

Interested
Water Lily Package ($125/session)

Package 3 includes weekly sessions, over a period of 12 weeks. All of the material covered in packages 1 and 2 will be included. Additionally, we will further explore childhood experiences and how they manifest in adult relationships, value-based living, limerence in marriage or a long-term partnership, making meaning after limerence, and a plan moving forward to sustain well-being and quality of life. This package will also incorporate use of an assessment tool in weeks 1, 6, and 12 to measure progress. After completing package 3, you will receive a discounted price for each future individual session, as needed. You will be eligible for monthly process groups, and you will have access to our private Facebook limerence group for ongoing support.

Interested
Monthly Process Groups ($40/month)

Those who complete the 12-week package are eligible to join our monthly process group for $40/month. We will be covering certain themes each week, in which individuals can share their experience in a safe and supportive environment. Building a limerence community, in which we can raise each other up, is so important! Monthly groups will go through a 12-month period and then begin again. Groups will consist of 6-8 people. Before our process group can begin, I will gather eligible clients and connect with you as to when the first group will take place.
